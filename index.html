About AJ

Hey there! I'm Amandeep Singh Jadhav, but everyone calls me AJ. My journey is a unique blend of creativity and technology, shaped by years of content creation and now focused on artificial intelligence and machine learning.

With over 650,000 subscribers on YouTube, I've mastered the art of storytelling, visual design, and audience engagement. But life had different plans – my channel faced challenges, but I never gave up. Instead, I transformed that setback into a comeback.

Currently pursuing AIML at Akal University (graduating 2028), I'm not just learning to code – I'm learning to innovate, to dream big, and to turn those dreams into reality. Every failure taught me resilience, every success taught me humility.
